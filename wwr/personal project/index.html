Casa De Jolly

Extraction of palm oil from fresh organic palmfruit which will be processed into consumable red palm oil, palm kernel oil,
and also manufactured into pomade,bathing soap and moisturizer for natural hair.

Our target is to create awareness to individuals, increase productivity as well as educate the public on its
health issues and why they should purchase the product for consumable and external purposes. It will be sold
on the website, mart,to individuals, retailers, shops for both local, and international purposes.
